# Structure Networks

## CableNetwork
`Assets.Scripts.Network.CableNetwork`

Transmits power and data between devices.

## AtmosphericsNetwork
`Networks.AtmosphericNetwork`

Simulates atmopheres contained in structures.

* `Assets.Scripts.Networks.PipeNetwork` for pipes and inline tanks
* `Networks.LandingPadNetwork` for trading gasses

## LandingPadNetwork
`Networks.LandingPadNetwork`

Connects runways and landing pads for use by traders (in addition to trading gas atmospherics).

## RocketNetwork
`Networks.RocketNetwork`

Connects rocket fuselages to make a complete rocket

## RoboticArmNetwork
`Objects.RobotArm.RoboticArmNetwork`

Connects LArRE docks, linear rails, and linear stations to form a complete track.

## ElevatorShaftNetwork
`Assets.Scripts.Objects.Electrical.ElevatorShaftNetwork`

Connects elevator levels and shaft pieces to make a complete elevator shaft.
